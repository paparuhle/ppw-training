New Hire Training Hub

Your 20-day path to product mastery. One hour a day, three phases. Sauna and hydrotherapy first, hyperbaric second, light therapy third.

Already Completed

You've already worked through the PPW Master Sales Framework, the Sauna Product Type Guide, and the Hyperbaric Chamber Product Type Guide. The PTG slots that would have been new-content days are now refresher + applied-scenario days that build on what you already learned.

How to use this hub

Each day is a focused block of material — roughly 45 to 75 minutes. Click into the day, read the overview, then work through the linked chapters in order. Everything opens in the existing guides so you see full context.

Phase 1 (Days 1–13) — Sauna Mastery. PTG refresher, applied scenarios + install drills, the Bathing Brands distributor call, heater selection logic, every sauna brand we carry, and hot tubs and cold plunges as the contrast-therapy companion.

Phase 2 (Days 14–19) — Hyperbaric Chambers. PTG refresher, applied scenarios + spec drills, brand-specific training, and a cross-sell stacking day.

Phase 3 (Day 20) — Light Therapy. Red light therapy. Smallest category, easy add-on, stacks beautifully with sauna.
