Cozy Heat — American Wood-Fire King

Estimated time: ~65 minutes · Source: cozy-heat-guide.html
Day 9 is Cozy Heat — our American wood-fire specialist. Different customer profile than Narvi (more 'I want the cabin experience', less 'I'm importing Finnish tradition'). Today you learn the two big universal decisions: Standard vs. Thru-Wall, and 12kW vs. 18kW.

What you'll learn today

  • Why Cozy Heat fits the American outdoor-sauna customer.
  • The five families: Cozy (base), O, OG, Quattro, SW.
  • Standard vs. Thru-Wall — the universal configuration decision.
  • 12kW vs. 18kW — the universal power decision.
  • Chimney and ventilation basics specific to wood-burning.

Why it matters

Cozy Heat is a volume brand for outdoor installs. Know the two universal decisions and you can recommend the right unit on any call within five minutes.
