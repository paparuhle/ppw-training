Huum — The BMW

Estimated time: ~75 minutes · Source: huum-guide.html
Day 6 is Huum — Estonian-made premium electric heaters. Think of them as the BMW of our lineup: serious performance, clean design, and a price that requires real sales skill. By end of today you should know all eight families and which customer each one fits.

What you'll learn today

  • Why we carry Huum and what makes them different.
  • All 8 families: CLIFF, DROP, STEEL, CORE, HIVE Mini, HIVE, HIVE Wood, HIVE Flow.
  • The UKU control system — the brain of every electric Huum.
  • The 12-question qualifying checklist mapped to Huum.
  • Huum-specific objection handling and the LAER framework.

Why it matters

Huum is one of our most-requested premium brands. A customer asking about Huum already knows what they want. Your job is to confirm the fit and move them to close without losing the sale to a competitor site.
