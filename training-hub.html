Peak Primal Wellness

New Hire Training Hub

Your 20-day path to product mastery. One hour a day, three phases. Sauna first, hyperbaric second, everything else third.

How to use this hub

Each day is a focused block of material — roughly 60 to 75 minutes of reading. Click into the day, read the overview, then work through the linked chapters in order. Everything opens in the existing guides so you see full context.

Phase 1 (Days 1–12) — Sauna Mastery. The full sauna product type guide, heater selection logic, and every sauna brand we carry.

Phase 2 (Days 13–18) — Hyperbaric Chambers. Our second most popular category. HBOT fundamentals followed by brand-specific training.

Phase 3 (Days 19–20) — Hydrotherapy & Light. Hot tubs, cold plunges, and red light therapy. Smaller categories but important for cross-selling.
