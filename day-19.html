Hot Tubs & Cold Plunges

Estimated time: ~55 minutes · Source: Hot_Tubs_Cold_Plunges_Product_Type_Guide.html
Phase 3 begins. Hot tubs and cold plunges together. Today you cover hydrotherapy fundamentals, the product categories in both, installation, water chemistry, and cross-selling plays with sauna customers.

What you'll learn today

  • The physiology of heat immersion vs. cold immersion — and why contrast therapy works.
  • Hot tub types: portable inflatable, traditional acrylic, wood-fired.
  • Cold plunge types: ice-fill, chiller-based, all-in-one combo.
  • Installation considerations for both, including foundation, electrical, plumbing.
  • Water maintenance for both hot tubs and cold plunges.
  • Cross-sell plays: hot tub + cold plunge, sauna + cold plunge, red light integration.

Why it matters

Cold plunge is one of the fastest-growing categories in wellness. Even if a customer came in for a sauna, they'll often add a cold plunge if you know how to offer it.
